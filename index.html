About

From Founder to Systems Specialist

I spent 20 years building and scaling Deshabille, a multi-channel ecommerce brand. Along the way, I became the person who solved the hard operational problems — integrating systems, automating workflows, managing ERP implementations and making technology work for the business, not against it.

That hands-on founder experience now drives my consulting work. I bring real commercial ownership to every engagement — understanding not just what needs to be built, but why it matters to the business. I specialise in ERP integrations, platform transformations and automation across Agile and Waterfall delivery environments.

I work with businesses who are ready to move from manual processes to scalable systems — and who need someone who can bridge the gap between business strategy and technical execution.
